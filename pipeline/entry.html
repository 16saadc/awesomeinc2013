Initiative Description
Awesome Inc
Lexington, KY
awesomeinc.org 		Exists to create and grow high tech startups by hosting community events, leading technology education courses and offering a shared workspace environment.
KY Innovation Network
13 KY Locations
kyinnovation.com 		Creates a network of business leaders and mentors to grow companies through the thirteen offices throughout Kentucky.
Startups@KSTC
Kentucky
startups.kstc.com 		Provides pre-seed and seed-stage funding and other resources to Kentucky-based companies with the goal of building successful technology-related companies in KY.
5 Across
Lexington, KY
5across.org 		Bi-monthly business pitch competition for entrepreneurs.
iNet
Lexington, KY
ci.uky.edu/inet 		Undergraduate academic program in leadership and entrepreneurial thinking for UK students that connects students to other students with entrepreneurial interests and to innovators and entrepreneurial mentors.
Startup Weekend
Lexington & Louisville, KY
startupweekend.org 		54-hour events where developers, designers, marketers, product managers and startup enthusiasts come together to share ideas, form teams, build products, and launch startups.
Idea State U
KY Colleges
ideastateu.com 		Kentucky’s statewide business plan competition designed to encourage innovation and entrepreneurship by rewarding student teams from the Commonwealth’s colleges and universities.
Small Business Innovation Research
National
sbir.gov 		Encourages Federal Research/Research and Development (R/R&D) that has the potential for commercialization. The Cabinet will match awards received by Kentucky high-tech small businesses and those willing to become Kentucky-based businesses.
Venture Studio
Lexington, KY
vace.uky.edu/venture-studio 		UK initiative that hosts a one semester program to assess and validate the feasibility of a business concept, create a business model, and provide hands-on experience with real world projects.
